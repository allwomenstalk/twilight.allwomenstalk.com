# 5 Reasons Why I Love Twilight ...

Twilight is a good book, no correction, it is a good series. I have read every one of the Twilight books and am now on The Short Second Life of Bree Tanner. Below, I am going to give 5 reasons why I love Twilight…

## 5. You Never Know What is Going to Happen Next

When I first started reading Twilight, it sucked me in. I like reading and I know a good book when I saw one. With Twilight, you never really know what is going to happen next. Throughout the story, I was wondering if Bella was going to end up with Jacob or Edward or if she was going to turn into a vampire.

## 4. The Abilities They Have

I like the special abilities they have. Each vampire has their own, unique abilities. That is nothing like Interview with a Vampire or any of those other cool vampire movies.

## 3. I like Vampire Movies

Needless to say, I am a big horror fan, so I like vampire movies. I have always been rather obsessed with vampires, so when Twilight came out, I jumped on it. I like the ones, such as Interview with a Vampire and Queen of the Damned.

## 2. I like Werewolf Movies

Ever since I was little, I liked werewolf movies. Of course, when I was little, werewolves scared me more than vampires did and I’m not sure why.

## 1. It’s a Good Story

Of course, if I didn’t think it was a good story, then I would not read it. I think Twilight is a good story. I definitely enjoyed reading the book and couldn’t wait for the movies to come out.

Those are my 5 reasons why I love Twilight. What about you? Why do you like Twilight so much? Besides, cute guys – what do you like about it?
