# 5 Reasons a Twilight Obsession is Not Good ...

This can be an obsession a wife has. The husband could feel like a wife feels when the husband has an obsession with pornography. The more they indulge, the more it pushes them away. I love Twilight, but I’m not going to be obsessed with it. I am going to give you 5 reasons a Twilight obsession is not good.

## #5

You Think About it All the Time. Come on. Thinking about it all the time is no good. It’s a book, read it and be done with it, don’t base your life on it.

## #4

Husbands will be less motivated to fulfill their wives. Why is this? This is because they know they will not be able to compete with a fantasy.

## #3

You’ll Be Lonely if you are obsessed over Twilight and base your men on Twilight. Face it, you are never going to find a guy that is like Edward, so don’t try waiting for him.

## #2

If Marriage is Already Weak, Filling the connection is not a good idea. When you fill the hole in a marriage with a fantasy, it will cause it to be even weaker.

## #1

Kids of Twilight moms say “my mom loves Jacob or Edward more than she loves my dad” is not a good site. Not to mention what it does to the kid emotionally, especially if they really believe this.

There you have 5 reasons a Twilight obsession is not good. I know some of you may think that I took this blog a bit too far, but I am only trying to speak the truth. If I offended any of you, I am sorry. I did not mean to rip apart your fantasy that you may have. So, do you believe me?
