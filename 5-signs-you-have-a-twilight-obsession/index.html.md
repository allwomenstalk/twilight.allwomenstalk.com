# 5 Signs You Have a Twilight Obsession ...

I have decided to give 5 signs you have a Twilight obsession. I want you to determine if you have one or not!

## 5. You Think a Lot about It

When you think a lot about something, then you are either addicted or obsessed. In this case, the book will not flow through your veins, so it is not an addiction. It is, however, an obsession.

## 4. Your Husband Roles His Eyes

Whenever you mention Twilight to your lovely husband, you see him role your eyes. This may be because you talk about it so much.

## 3. You Base Your Real Life on This Fantasy

You are looking for Edward. You are waiting for that Edward to show up. If you do this, then you are going to be alone for the rest of your life. Edward does not exist and if you do not see that, then you are obsessed.

## 2. It’s the Only Book You Read

Ever since it first came out, you have not read any other books. Twilight is the only book that you have read. This is an obsession.

## 1. You Write Twilight Names All over the Place

You know, Jacob, Bella, Edward, Rosalie, Charlie and all of those names, right? If you have them written all over your folders, then you are obsessed with it.

Girl, come on, get a hold of yourself! Twilight is a fantasy, it is not meant to be real life and Stephanie Meyer did not mean for it to be thought of as real life. Just kidding. You do what you want to do. If you like Twilight, then like Twilight. However, make sure you read other books! What other books have you read?
