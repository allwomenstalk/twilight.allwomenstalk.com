Have you heard of this one before? Well, it is true. Ghosts really do not have any sense of the time that is passing. One hundred years could pass and they would not even realize it. In a way, this is a sad fact.

That’s right, ghosts are able to smell things. In fact, it is said that they enjoy the smell of lemons. So, if you have any lemons around your house, the ghosts surely love it.
