# 5 Things to Remember when You See Robert in Hollywood ...

When you meet Robert Pattinson , there are some things you will need to remember to do. Right now, I am going to give you 5 things to remember when you see Robert in Hollywood …

## 5. Don’t Lose Your Mind

Psst, hey, try not to lose your mind over there. Don’t jump up and down and scream – you might scare him away. You wouldn’t want that now would you?

## 4. They Are Normal People Too

Robert is a normal person. Yes, he may be a celebrity, but nonetheless, he eats, breaths in air and sleeps – he is just like you. He has happy moments and he has sad moments (Yes, Robert has sad moments). He even has angry moments and he makes mistakes from time to time. Remember, he is only human.

## 3. Take Three Deep Breaths

This way, you will not faint. You know, because when you see Robert, from Twilight, you may feel as if you are going to hit the floor. You wouldn’t want to do that in front of him, would you?

## 2. Make Conversation

Try making conversation with him. You may be surprised when he talks back to you. Remember the tip I gave you in number 4, they are normal people too. Act as if you are talking to a normal person.

## 1. Give Yourself a Pat on the Back

But only do this if you are good. If you did horrible and made a goof of yourself when you met him, then don’t give yourself a pat on the back. However, keep in mind that many girls go crazy whenever they see Robert, so you are not alone. SO, in that case, give yourself a pat on the back for getting to see him.

Those are 5 things to remember when you see Robert in Hollywood. So, how many of you have gotten the chance to see him in real life?
