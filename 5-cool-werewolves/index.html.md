# 5 Cool Werewolves ...

I always thought werewolves were cool. Then again, I also always thought vampires were cool. I guess you could say that I have always had a taste for those unique creatures. Right now, I want to tell you 5 cool werewolves …

## 5. Anna

Anna comes from a werewolf in a book called “Cry Wolf.” Anna is a different type of vampire. She is an Omega. Have any of you read this book?

## 4. Quil Ateara

This is a werewolf that starred in the ever so popular Twilight series. Quil Ateara was Jacob Black’s werewolf friend.

## 3. Ginger

Ginger was a popular werewolf that started in Ginger Snaps 1 and 2.

## 2. Brigitte

Brigitte in Ginger Snaps 1 and 2 also became a werewolf. Both Brigitte and Ginger were fun to watch in the movie. You didn’t know what was going to happen next.

## 1. Jacob Black

Yes, good old Jacob Black from the Twilight series. Even though he smells too much like a wet dog, he is still an awesome werewolf that we all learned to love (or to hate).

Those are 5 cool werewolves. I can actually think of many other cool werewolves. What about you? Can you name some other werewolves? I know it’s hard to put a name to some of them as they’re creatures who kill in movies and they usually do not have a name.
