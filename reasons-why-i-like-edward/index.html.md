# Reasons Why I like Edward

Wow, I don’t know about you girls who chose Jacob! I mean, come on, do you not realize what Edward has? What Edward could give you? I don’t see who in their right mind would have chosen to be on Team Jacob. Then again, I am sure those girls that chose Team Jacob do not understand why any girl in their right mind would have chosen Team Edward. Okay, well, I have some reasons why I like Edward (his character) and that is what I am going to tell you about in this blog.

## #1

First of all, I like how he goes out of his way for Bella. When Bella wants to see Jacob, he drops her off at the treaty line to meet him and then picks her up.

## #2

Did I mention that he sparkles in the sunlight? Wow, I wish I could sparkle every time the sunlight shines on me. The only way I could do that would be applying a lot of glitter to myself.

## #3

I also like how he is old fashioned. He doesn’t force himself on Bella like a lot of other guys would. He seems to do everything the right way.

## #4

He stares at you while you’re sleeping. I always thought that was cute.

## #5

He doesn’t sleep! Wow, he doesn’t sleep – isn’t that a reason to like him?

## #6

He’s not selfish. Don’t we all like guys who are not selfish?

## #7

He has a car that is only for special occasions.

## #8

He will never let you go and he will love you forever.

## #9

He’s a vampire!

## #10

Because he lives forever!

## #11

Last but not least, because he is not a werewolf! Haha Jacob!

Do you know something? I could continue forever with this post! I bet I could easily come up with one hundred ways! Add to it!
