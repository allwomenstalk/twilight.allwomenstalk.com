# 5 Unforgettable Scenes from Twilight That Still Give Us Chills

There was definitely plenty of action in the Twilight series. I thought each book had a great combination of action, romance, and general story telling. While it was hard to choose, the 5 mind grabbing scenes from the Twilight series that I recall the most are described below in detail. If you are still reading the books, then you might not want to continue reading what I’ve listed below.

Whether you're a long-time fan or just discovering these **twilight scenes** for the first time, some moments just stick with you. These **scenes from twilight** aren't just about the plot—they're the reason we fell in love with the series. If you're looking for more, check out these [reasons we will always love the Twilight saga](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-we-will-always-love-the-twilight-saga/).

## 5. The Rush to Save Edward

Even though I couldn’t imagine any harm ever coming to Edward, it was still suspenseful to read about Bella and Alice’s flight to stop him from self-destruction. I kept waiting for the Volturi to step in at the very last minute and foil the plan. For more details on the cast and production of this high-stakes moment, visit the [New Moon IMDB page](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1259571/). This is definitely one of the **twilight best scenes** for its sheer tension.

## 4. Birth of the Baby

The birthing process was very graphic and horrific. Bella had already gone through so much just carrying the infant. I was glad when it was all over and everyone was doing well. Talk about suspense! This pivotal moment in _Breaking Dawn_ changed everything for the Cullens. You can read more about the [lore of Renesmee](https://twilightsaga.fandom.com/wiki/Renesmee_Cullen) on the official fandom wiki.

## 3. Bella’s Near-Death Experience with the Van

I think my mouth dropped open when I read this part of the book. Actually, I think I read it twice! I just couldn’t imagine anything happening to Bella right at the beginning of the series, but I definitely didn’t expect Edward to step in so soon. This is one of those classic **twilight clips** that still gets shared everywhere. ## 2. Battle with the Newborns

The most detailed portion of this battle is the fight between Edward and Victoria. There was a lot of suspense. I remember gripping the book as I was reading this part. The decapitation at the end of the battle was priceless. It's a standout moment in the [official Eclipse story](https://www.stepheniemeyer.com/the-books/eclipse/), showing just how far Edward would go to protect Bella.

## 1. Meeting the Volturi

Reading about the reaction this group of vampires had on other vampires was very interesting. I’d had the feeling that nothing really seemed to faze vampires, until I read about the meeting between the Cullens and the Volturi. Their introduction brought a level of ancient authority and menace that really upped the stakes for the rest of the series. While the [rush to save Edward](#5) showed their power, this meeting was where we truly felt the danger.
