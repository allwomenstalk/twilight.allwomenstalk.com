# Top 5 Scary Creatures ...

I have always had an eye for scary creatures. Perhaps that is why I like horror movies so much? Right now, I am going to give you the top 5 scary creatures …

## 5. The Vampire

The vampire is definitely a scary creature to many. Some people actually have fears of this creature. If you are a Twilight fan, then you probably do not think of them as scary. Instead, you probably think of them as being handsome and glittery.

## 4. The Werewolf

Oh yes, I remember having fears of the werewolf visiting me when I was a little girl. Fortunately, to this day, I have not gotten the chance to be visited by a werewolf. These are definitely scary creatures.

## 3. The Goat Sucker

Do you know what I find to be so scary about these creatures? The fact that even though they are being researched, we still have not gotten the chance to see one up close and personal. There’s actually a chance that the goat sucker is real and walking through our woods, yet we do not see him/her.

## 2. Goblins

Goblins were made famous by fairy tales, so surely, they shouldn’t be scary right? Oh, you have that one wrong. There are many horror movies that now have goblins in them. These creatures definitely deserve to be on my list.

## 1. Witches

How could we forget about the green creatures with pointy hats, pointy nose and warts? Yes, Hansel and Gretel may have shoved one in the oven, but they did not shove them all. Many people, to this day, are afraid of witches.

Those are the top 5 scary creatures, that in my opinion, deserve to be talked about. If you enjoy scary creatures just as much as I do, then I urge you to tell us any others that you may have on your list.
