# 5 Hot Vampire Guys from the Twilight Series ...

I never understood what the infatuation with vampires was, but even as a young child, while most little girls were afraid at the thought of vampires, I liked vampires. Vampires were always fascinating to me and when I read the first series of Twilight, I thought that it was different from any other vampire story I have ever read. I mean, come on, where else do you find sparkly vegetarian vampires? Vampires who do not prey on humans. I did not know these kind of things even existed. Then again, when it comes to imagination, everything can exist. Anyhow, I wanted to tell you about 5 [hot guys](https://love.allwomenstalk.com/hot-guys-which-one-is-your-type/) from the Twilight Series. I am basing this all on the book and not on the characters that were selected to play Twilight in the movies.

## 5. James

I would have to say that this is the bad guy from the Twilight series, but that doesn’t mean he’s not hot! Alice was transformed by a vampire that worked at the asylum. This was done in order to protect her from James. James is a tracker vampire who was hunting her.

## 4. Carlisle

In the book, Carlisle is described as looking like a model. He is Esme’s husband and Rosalie, Edward, Alice, Emmett and Jasper’s adoptive father. His physical age is only 23. He has blond hair and a slender, yet muscular body.

## 3. Emmett

Emmett is Rosalie’s husband. He is Carlisle and Esme’s adopted son as well as Jasper’s, Alice and Edward’s adopted brother. He is described as being burly, tall and very muscular. He has dimpled cheeks and slightly curly hair. In the Cullens, he is the jokester and he is always eager when it comes to fighting.

## 2. Jasper

Jasper is the adopted son of Esme and Carlisle. He is the adoptive brother of Emmet, Rosalie and Edward. He is the husband of Alice. He takes on the last name Hale so that he can pass as being Rosalie’s sibling. He joined the Confederate States Army in 1861 in order to serve in the Civil War. He went through ranks quickly. He was turned into a vampire in 1863 by Maria when he was twenty years old. He is able to manipulate emotions of those around him.

## 1. Edward

Now, Edward is the main guy to many people. They love how he sparkles in the book and I must say, the description is pretty hot. He was born on June 20 of 1901 and is frozen in his seventeen year old body. He was dying of the Spanish influenza when he was changed into a vampire by Dr. Carlisle Cullen. This is the guy that Bella goes crazy over in the book! This is the guy she goes out of her mind for. Do you blame her?

There you have the 5 hot vampire guys from the Twilight series. Of course, if you know of any other vampire guys that you would like to add to the list, just place that in the comment section of this blog!
