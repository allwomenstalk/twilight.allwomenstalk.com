# 5 Unexpected Reasons to Love Zombies

Zombies, they are such lovely or not so lovely creatures. They're the walking dead and they are the third best thing! The first best is vampires and the second best is werewolves. Let me tell you 5 reasons to love zombies. The undead have captured our imagination for decades, lumbering through pop culture and leaving a lasting impression. Whether you are a fan of classic horror or modern thrillers, there is something undeniably fascinating about these creatures.

Before we dive in, let us look at some of the most iconic ways zombies have appeared in media:

- Classic slow-moving ghouls

- Fast and aggressive infected

- Comedic and self-aware undead

## 5. They Don't Care What People Think

I really look up to zombies. Do you know why? Because they don't give a heck what someone thinks of them. You can sit there and call them all kinds of bad names to their face and they are just going to look at you and try to get through the fence. You can even shoot at them, their feelings are not going to be hurt. In a world full of social anxiety, there is something refreshing about a creature that just focuses on its single goal without worrying about appearances or opinions.

## 4. They Don't Care What They Eat

Well, as long as it is meat and blood, they don't seem to care what they eat. They are certainly not picky eaters. While we stress over dietary restrictions, meal planning, and finding the perfect restaurant, zombies keep it incredibly simple. They are the ultimate pragmatists when it comes to dining, never complaining about the menu. If you are looking for more interesting takes on monsters, you might want to explore [why vampires are so popular](/5-reasons-to-love-vampires/), as they also have very specific dietary needs.

## 3. They do Funny Things

Have you ever watched a funny zombie movie? You have to admit, some zombies do funny things. Movies like [Shaun of the Dead](https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/shaun_of_the_dead) perfectly capture the comedic potential of the walking dead. From tripping over their own feet to failing at basic tasks, their clumsy nature often provides excellent physical comedy. It is hard not to chuckle when a supposed terrifying monster bumps into a glass door.

## 2. They Don't Talk

Okay, I know that communication is always the key, but that isn't so with zombies. You see, when you are a zombie, you no longer have that desire to talk. All you would care about is … being destructive? There are no awkward silences, no small talk, and no complicated arguments. Just simple groans and a straightforward attitude. For those who appreciate the quiet life, a zombie's lack of conversation might actually seem like a peaceful change of pace. Plus, not talking means they never have to explain why they [are not picky eaters](#4) either.

## 1. They Don't Care about Valentine's Day

Oh yes, all of you guys are going to love this one! You want to date a zombie girl. They aren't going to nag you about gifts. They won't even care if you do not get them chocolate for Valentine's Day! The concept of a love day zombies would celebrate is hilarious because they simply do not care about romance. Sorry, I just had to include this one as number one, I just couldn't help myself! Their complete apathy towards commercial holidays is honestly something we can all learn from.

The genre has expanded so much, from terrifying apocalyptic nightmares to lighthearted comedies. If you want to dive deeper into the lore, check out the [Zombie Wiki](https://zombie.fandom.com/wiki/Zombie_Wiki) for endless details on different variations. So, do you love or hate zombies?
