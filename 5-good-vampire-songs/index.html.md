# 5 Good Vampire Songs ...

Hmm … I could only stop and wonder what type of music a vampire would listen to. Vampires love music, but what type of music would they like? I definitely do not think they would want to listen to country or any type of pop music. They seem more like classical music lovers mixed in with rock lovers. I am not only going to give you 5 good vampire songs, but I am going to give you 5 good vampire songs that I like too …

## 5. Queen of the Damned – System

This is a pretty good song. I find myself listening to it in the car from time to time and then the tune stays stuck in my mind.

## 4. Marilyn Manson – Sweet Dreams

Yes, Sweet Dreams are made of these. I definitely had to include this one on the list. Somehow, I think a vampire would like Marilyn. I know I do.

## 3. Marilyn Manson – This is Halloween

Oh come on, you can’t blame me for putting this song on here too! It is one of my favorites and ‘tis the time to listen to it!

## 2. Moonlight Sonata – Beethoven

Yes, this is definitely another song I have to include on this list. It sounds like something a vampire would love. I not only like listening to it, I like playing it.

## 1. Godsmack – Voodoo

This is a cool song. It’s just one of those songs I could listen to over and over again. Godsmack has always been a favorite of mine.

So, there you have 5 god vampire songs.What else would you add to this list?
