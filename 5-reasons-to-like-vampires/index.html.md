# 5 Reasons to like Vampires ...

I always had this odd attraction to vampires. I find them to be interesting creatures that I cannot get enough of. Do you think vampires find humans as interesting as we find them? Either way, I am going to give you 5 reasons to like vampires ….

## 5. They’re Mysterious

Vampires are definitely mysterious creatures that we as humans cannot get enough of. Don’t you wish you could be a vampire? Now, don’t go psycho on me and swear you are one by drinking blood. Chances are, you’re not a vampire.

## 4. They Have a Dark Side

Oh yeah, they have a dark side and you know how girls crave those bad boys. Vampires let that dark side show. I must also add that I love how their teeth look.

## 3. They Sound Good in Books

Of course, they sound good in books. Nearly every vampire I read about in a book has an interesting trait. Think about Edward, he sounded good in the book, don’t you think?

## 2. They Live Forever

Yes, vampires do have a tendency to live forever and this is one of the things I like about them. They never have to face death. The only bad thing about this is the fact that they have to watch those around them die, which is sad.

## 1. They Love You Eternally

Wouldn’t it be amazing to have an eternal love that never ends? I think so too and this is why I listed it amongst my 5 reasons to like vampires.

Those are my 5 reasons to like vampires. I admit that I have always liked vampires, even as a kid. No, I was not scared of them. What about you, what do you like about them?
