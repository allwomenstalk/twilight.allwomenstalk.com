# 5 Facts on Druids ...

I have been doing a bit of research on certain things in the paranormal world lately. I was playing World of Warcraft the other night when I had the urge to go do some research on druids. I have found that druids are actually quite interesting to say the least. I’m sure that some of my readers are going to agree with me on this one. Did you come here for some facts? Then get ready for 5 facts on druids …

## 5. Animals

Druids will not harm any animals in any way. They offer things that come from nature, including flowers, fruits and vegetables. This is one of the things I really like about druids.

## 4. Common Symbol

Even a druid has a common symbol that they may wear. In fact, they have a couple of common symbols. Two of the common symbols include the Triskellion and the Druid Sigil. The Triskellion is a simple design of acorns and oak leaves. The Druid Sigil consists of a circle with two lines going through it.

## 3. Prayers

Some druids may choose to recite or sing prayers. These prayers are either in Celtic or Gaelic languages. Both of these are beautiful languages.

## 2. What Sex Can They Be

A druid can be either a male or a female.

## 1. What Interests Druids

Most druids have a love of intellectual growth, history and creativity. They may also be interested in the pursuit of truth and justice.
