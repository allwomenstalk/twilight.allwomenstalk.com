# 5 Things about Stephanie Meyer You Should Know ...

If you are interested in Twilight, then you should be interested in the mastermind behind it. Below, I am going to give you 5 things about Stephanie Meyer you should know …

## 5. Her Dream

She stated that she first came up with Twilight because of a very vivid dream she had. She had a dream one night of a girl and a boy sitting in a meadow having an in depth conversation and after that, she went to write a book.

## 4. She Was a Stay at Home Mom

Before June 2, 2003, before she started writing Twilight, Stephanie Meyer was just a stay at home mother of 3 boys.

## 3. Where Did Stephanie Meyer Graduate from?

Stephanie Meyer may have been a stay at home mother, but she did get the chance to graduate from college. She graduated from Brigham Young University. She got a bachelor’s degree in English.

## 2. Of 2008 and 2009, She Was the Biggest Selling Author

Of course, with all the teenage girls out there, wouldn’t you think she sold a lot of copies? During the year 2008 alone, she sold over twenty nine copies. Now, that is a lot!

## 1. When Did She Meet Her Husband?

She met her husband in Arizona, when she was growing up. They got married in 1994. They were both 21 at the time. They have three sons together, Gabe, Seth and Eli. Her husband is Christian Meyer. He was formerly an auditor. However, he has retired to stay home and take care of their children.

Those are 5 things about Stephanie Meyer you should know. I think it is always interesting to read about the writer after you have read the book. Do you know something about Stephanie that I did not list in this blog? If so, please share!
