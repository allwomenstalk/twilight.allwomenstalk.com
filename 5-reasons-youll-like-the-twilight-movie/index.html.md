# 5 Reasons Youll like the Twilight Movie ...

Oh yeah! You have to love Twilight! Yeah, okay, so what are some reasons you’ll like Twilight? Admit it, you are not pulled into Twilight like some of the people, mostly girls, are. Therefore, you need some reasons as to why you’ll like the Twilight movie. Well, really, I do not know your personal taste, so I am going to try to base it on different things and not just on why I like it. Below, I am going to give you 5 reasons you’ll like the Twilight movie.

## 5. Boys

Yeah, okay. I have to include this one, because girls like boys and well, you know, Twilight has some pretty nice boys in it.

## 4. Edward

I think Edward gives us a good reason to watch the Twilight movie and I am not going to say any more about this, because I don’t want to ruin it for you.

## 3. The Love

Yeah, when you watch the Twilight movies, you can just feel the love. Love is coming from all different directions – from mothers, fathers, friends, boyfriends, sisters, etc.

## 2. Vampires

Oh yeah, there is vampires in this movie! Isn’t this reason enough to watch it? Of course, it’s not the crappy type of vampire movie that bases every second on sex. This is the good, clean type of vampire movie. I think you’ll like it if you like vampires. It’s a twist to things and that is for sure.

## 1. The Drama

Oh yeah, in this movie, there is definitely drama. For all of you drama queens out there, you are going to enjoy this movie. Of course, the drama isn’t everything here. There are many more good things about Twilight.

Those are 5 reasons you’ll like the Twilight movie. If you read the book, then you have to watch the movie! What do you think?
