# 5 Cool Creatures to Be during Halloween ...

Halloween is right around the corner. It is my favorite time of the year and I am happy to write Halloween blogs for you. I am going to give you 5 cool creatures to be during Halloween …

## 1. A Butterfly

If you are going for the girly look, then you could dress up as a butterfly. The butterfly is beautiful. However, you could turn yourself into a butterfly that came back from the dead. You know, after it was hit by a car.

## 2. A Night Elf

Of yeah, I think of the Night Elf as a creature. I am talking about a Night Elf from World of Warcraft. I must say that this is something fun to dress up as.

## 3. A Mummy

Do you know how popular the mummy is? I remember when I was a child, one Halloween, my parents dressed me up as a mummy. It was such a fun costume to wear. This is definitely a cool creature to be during Halloween.

## 4. A Werewolf

Yes, a werewolf would definitely be a cool creature to be during Halloween. If you have a taste for werewolves, then you should try being one for Halloween.

## 5. Vampire

Of course, the vampire got the number one spot on this list. It would be wrong not to list the vampire as the number one creature to be during Halloween. This is, after all, a blog that is based on vampires!

Those are 5 cool creatures to be during Halloween. Can you think of any other cool creatures?
