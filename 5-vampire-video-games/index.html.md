# 5 Vampire Video Games ...

I have a huge collection of games. I am not only a gamer, but I am also a collector. Amongst my collection of video games, you are going to find many vampire type of games. I like to keep my games organized, so all of my vampire games are put together. If you are interested in knowing what type of vampire games there are, here are 5 vampire video games …

## 5. Vampire: the Masquerade

This is a cool game, depending on the type of games you enjoy. With this one, players will be controlling a vampire along with a party of up to 3 others. This is a 3d game where you will be playing against other vampires, werewolves and some other creatures.

## 4. Legacy of Kain

This series first started as an action role-playing game. The vampires name is Kain – Kain is a playable character.

## 3. Bloodrayne

This is a third person action game that you are going to enjoy if you like vampire games as well as other supernatural.

## 2. Buffy the Vampire Slayer

Do you like Buffy the Vampire Slayer? If you do, then you are going to like the video game. In the video game, you will have the option to control Buffy. Yes, it’s time to kick some vampire butt.

## 1. Castlevania

Castlevania is one of my favorite vampire games. In fact, I remember playing it as a child on the Nintendo system. Now, today, you will find it on the newer systems. Yes, it is definitely something you will find in my list of games.

Those are 5 vampire video games that I have in my collection of games. From time to time, I pull them out and play them. My daughter also enjoys them. So, what about you? Do you enjoy playing vampire games?
