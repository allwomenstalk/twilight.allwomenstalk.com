# 5 More Awesome Vampire Movies ...

Do you like vampire movies? If you like Twilight, then surely, there are some other vampire movies out there that you would enjoy. Right now, I can think of 5 more awesome vampire movies that are worth seeing. Would you like me to give you the list? Okay, here you go …

## 5. From Dusk Till Dawn (1996)

I know, this movie was filmed in 1996, but sometimes, old movies are the best, especially when it comes to vampire movies. George Clooney and Quentin Tarantino come together in this ever so popular vampire movie.

## 4. Underworld (2003)

Again, Underworld is really a cool movie. This is one of those vampire VS. Werewolves sagas that you just can’t miss out on. If you have not saw it before, then you have to get it today.

## 3. Blade (1998)

How can I forget to add Blade to this list? This was a really cool movie and I have watched it a ton of times before. This vampire hunter really does kick butt!

## 2. Van Helsing (2004)

Have you not got the chance to watch Van Helsing yet? Then you are 6 years behind! Watch Hugh Jackman as the legendary Van Helsing today!

## 1. Dracula (1931)

Don’t have a love to those black and white scenes? Well, Dracula should be an exception to that rule. We just can’t forget about the original Dracula!

There you have 5 more awesome vampire movies. There are many other movies out there that are based on vampires that I know you would like. How many of them have you watched?
