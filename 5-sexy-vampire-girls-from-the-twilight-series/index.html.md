## 5. Irina Denali

Irina is a Vampire from the Twilight series. She is one of the original Denali sisters. She is the originator of the myth of the succubus. She lead a peaceful lifestyle as she was a “vegetarian.” Just like her sisters, she loved men, all men. Before Laurent was killed, she had a relationship with him. After giving false information to the Volturi about Renesmee Cullen, she was killed.

## 4. Kate Denali

Her name was Katrina, but they called her Kate. She is also a vegetarian vampire and is one of the original Denali sisters. She has a strong relationship with her Denali coven, but she also has a relationship with the Olympic Coven. She has a strong mind and the talent of raising a mental electric current on her skin, which shocks anyone who contacts her.

## 3. Alice Cullen

Alice Cullen is an old vampire! She was born in 1901, but you can’t tell. You now, because vampires freeze in their age. She is the wife of Jasper Hale and adopted daughter of Esme and Carlisle Cullen. She is the adoptive sister of Alice, Rosalie, Edward and Emmet. She is the adoptive sister in law of Bella Cullen and the aunt of Renessmee Cullen. She always liked Bella, unlike Rosalie.
