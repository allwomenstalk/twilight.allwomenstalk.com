# 5 Reasons I like Werewolf Movies ...

I like werewolf movies, just as I like vampire movies. Today, I am going to give you 5 reasons I like werewolf movies.

## 5. They’re Funny

Some of the werewolf movies are actually funny and I actually like when a horror movie makes me laugh. Take Twilight as an example, I loved those wet dog jokes that were always aimed towards Jacob.

## 4. They Scare Me

I do not think a horror movie is awesome until it officially gives me the heebie jeebies. When something gives me the heebie jeebies, then I definitely like the movie. For some odd reasons, werewolves are the one true creature that scares me. There is so much mystery sitting behind them. Are they real? Are they made up? Have they ever walked the land?

## 3. It’s Not All about Sex

I do not know about you, but I don’t like those movies that are all based on sex. With werewolves, they’re not based on sex. In fact, we never see a feast of werewolves mating in a bed – no offense against vampires.

## 2. They Are Secretive Creatures

I like watching werewolf movies, because the wolves are secretive creatures. They are usually humans that turn into wolves and you never really know who the wolf is, until the story is revealed.

## 1. Perfect Setting

I believe werewolf movies always host that perfect setting. So, grab a thing of popcorn, a cold bottle of water and start watching them! They are great to watch with a guy!

Those are my 5 reasons I like werewolf movies. Some of you may not like werewolf movies, but that is okay. Werewolves aren’t for everyone. Do you like werewolves?
