# 5 Spooky Pumpkin Designs ...

Pumpkin carving is definitely an art and it is something that I enjoy doing. Did you know that there are actually pumpkin carving contests out there? I have always wanted to enter my pumpkins into one, but I never get the chance to. Below, I am going to talk about 5 spooky pumpkin designs.

## 5. Vampire Carved

There are many different varieties of this, so any type would be cool. You would take a pumpkin and carve a spooky vampire face into it. You could print something from your computer and use it as a stencil.

## 4. Classic Black Cat

We have all came across this and to this day, I still like it, no matter how many times I have done it. You know, having a cat arched with his hair showing carved into a pumpkin – yes, that is definitely a classic.

## 3. Frankenstein

Frankenstein is a pretty scary guy, don’t you think? You could easily print a picture from your computer and use it as a stencil.

## 2. An Owl

You could actually do an owl face on the pumpkin. This is definitely something that is spooky looking, if you do it right.

## 1. Haunted House

Carving a haunted house on a pumpkin would be great. Look it up and you will get many different ideas.

Those are 5 spooky pumpkin designs that I like. There are many different pumpkin designs and you should remember that you will never mess up. It will be unique. It is not going to be perfect. What design do you like?
