# 5 Reasons to Read the Entire Series of Twilight ...

Why would you not want to finish reading Twilight? If you liked it, then you should read the entire series. Below, I am going to give you 5 reasons to read the entire series of Twilight …

## 5. It Will Last Longer

When you read all of the series of Twilight, it is going to last longer. I couldn’t imagine just reading the first book of Twilight and stopping there.

## 4. You’ll Know the Full Story

I tell you, you need to know the full story of Twilight.

## 3. You Cannot Be a True Twilight Fan

Until you have read the entire series of Twilight, you cannot be a true Twilight fan. In order to be a true Twilight fan, you have to know everything that went on!

## 2. You Can Talk about It with Your Friends

If you have friends, then chances are, they like Twilight as well. More than likely, they read the full series by now. If you are the only one left that has not read the full series, then do you think you will be able to talk about it with your friends? No. When they do talk about it, you will be excluded from the conversation simply because you will be lost and do not know what is going on.

## 1. You’ll Miss out on the Ending

I tell you, the ending in Breaking Dawn is definitely something to look forward to. It will be something that will shock you. Face it, the first book of Twilight is only the beginning. Once you are done reading that first book, you are nowhere close to reading that story. There is so much left that you need to learn.

There is 5 reasons why you should read the entire Twilight series. Why do you think you should read the entire Twilight series?
