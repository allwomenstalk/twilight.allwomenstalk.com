# 5 Reasons to Read Twilight ...

How many of you here enjoyed reading Twilight? I enjoyed the entire series, even the book on Bree Tanner. Below, I am going to give you 5 reasons to read Twilight …

## 5. There’s Werewolves

Of course, we can’t forget about the dogs in this book – Opps, sorry, I meant to say – we can’t forget about the werewolves in this book. Twilight definitely has some pretty cool dogs – I mean werewolves!

## 4. There’s Vampires

I really like vampires and this book has them! Not only do they have vampires, but they have vampires that sparkle. These vampires have special abilities, such as seeing the future and mind reading.

## 3. It Leaves You Wanting to Keep Reading

Do you know what I liked about Twilight? This book left me wanting to keep reading! Of course, this is good, but it prevented me from sleeping at night. I just couldn’t put the book down and when I did, I wanted to keep going back to it.

## 2. Reading is Fun

Of course, I have to say that it is not all about the book. You should read Twilight simply because reading is fun! Each time you read, it is as if you are going on one big adventure. You like going on adventures, right?

## 1. There’s Edward and Bella

Come on, they are the next Romeo and Juliette – of course, it’s not going to end like Romeo and Juliette did, but you can tell that they love one another. This is a perfect reason to read Twilight, don’t you think?

There you have 5 reasons to read Twilight. These are all of my reasons and I am curious as to hear why you read Twilight. Other than the boys that are in it – why did you like reading this book so much?
