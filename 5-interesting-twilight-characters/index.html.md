# 5 Interesting Twilight Characters ...

When I think about Twilight, I think about all of the interesting characters that were in the Twilight series. You had both vampires, werewolves and humans all mingled together. Below, I am going to give you 5 interesting Twilight characters…

## 5. Victoria

Victoria is interesting. In Eclipse, it’s interesting to see the depths she goes through in order to get back at Edward for killing her partner, James. She creates an army of newborn vampires. Now, if that isn’t love, I don’t know what isn’t!

## 4. The Volturi

While this does not count as a character, I would still have to add the Volturi, because they are a group of characters. All of them are unique and very powerful. The volturi is actually a powerful coven of vampires. It’s a lot like royalty to us humans.

## 3. Jacob

Jacob is interesting. No, I’m not going by his looks, I’m going by the way he acts. He loves Bella…until the end. Until he finally imprints on someone. Who he imprints on shocks me. Does it shock you?

## 2. Renesmee

Renesmee is very interesting. She’s cute, too. She’s a symbol of Edward and Bella’s love. Renesmee always made me laugh. I like how she can touch someone and let them see what she knows.

## 1. Edward

Yeah, yeah, you knew I was going to put Edward as number one. Haha. Yeah, if there wasn’t an Edward, then I don’t think Twilight would be as interesting. Go Team Edward!

In my opinion, those are 5 interesting Twilight characters. There are many other characters that played in the twilight series that I did not mention on her and I still think they are interesting. Would you mind naming some of the Twilight characters that I did not include on this list that you believe should have been on here?
