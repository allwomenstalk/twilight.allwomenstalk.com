# 5 Popular Vampires from Movies ...

Vampires are mythological creatures that have a love for blood. Vampires are also popular in Hollywood. I have 5 popular vampires from movies that I would like to tell you about.

## 5. Edward

Oh yeah, who can’t forget about Edward? Edward is from Twilight. It seems many girls love him and many guys want to be him.

## 4. Angel

I am talking about the Angel that comes from the popular TV series Buffy the Vampire Slayer. Later, he went on his own spinoff series called Angel.

## 3. Felipe De Castro

Felipe De Castro comes from Sookie Stackhouse. The True Blood book and television series. Do any of you know of him?

## 2. Spike

Do any of you remember Spike? Spike comes from the popular television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

## 1. Nosferatu

Of course, many of you remember Nosferatu, right? He first appeared during the year 1922 in a movie called Nosferatu. It was an adaptation of Dracula.
