# 5 Haunted Places ...

I have always been interested in haunted placed. There are various places in my area that I believe are haunted. I live between Jacksonville and Gainesville, Florida. However, I am going to venture out of my town and tell you 5 haunted places that I would like to visit one day.

## 5. The Fayette County Courthouse

This is a courthouse that is located off of National Road US 40. It is in Uniontown, Pennsylvania, if any of you are interested in visiting it. It is said to be haunted. Legend says that they used to hand people from the balcony on the clocktower at the courthouse.

## 4. Alcatraz

Alcatraz was used as a prison from the year 1850 to 1963. It was used as a prison before the military started using it in 1850, actually. Native American tribes have used this as a prison. Either way you stack it, if you like things that are haunted, then this would be a great place to visit.

## 3. Brazil

The Joelma Building in Sao Paulo is said to be haunted. It is said that the victims of a fire that started on February 1, 1974 are still haunting the building. The fire started after a air unit on the twelfth floor got too hot. It is famous for the Mystery of the Thirteen Sould.

## 2. Bhangarh, Rajasthan

It is said that Bhangarh, Rajasthan is haunted. This haunting started during the first half of the 17th century.

## 1. Malaysia

Have you ever heard of Genting Highlands? This is a city that can be found in Pahang. It is known for a casino and amusement park. A couple of people have committed suicide here and it is said that they are haunting it today.

There you have 5 haunted places that would be interesting to see. Would you like to name more?
