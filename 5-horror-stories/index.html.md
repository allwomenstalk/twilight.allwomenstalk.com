# 5 Horror Stories ...

I enjoy horror stores – that is my favorite category. If you are reading this blog right now, then you are probably wondering if I am going to have any horror stories to tell you about. I am going to tell you of 5 horror stores …

## 5. Under the Dome by Stephen King

In this book, a tiny New England town is cut off from the entire world. This traps some characters inside (or outside) of a big, clear dome. As the story unfolds, various people emerge to take part in this drama. What is the dome? Is the town going to survive? Why is it there?

## 4. Trapped (a Novel of Terror) by Jack Kilborn

This is the follow up to “Afraid.” It all started with a harmless camping trip. A time to bond and a time to heal on Michigan’s upper peninsula. Then, Martin told everyone a campfire story about the history of the island. They laughed when Martin pretended he was being dragged off into the woods …

## 3. Torment- a Novel of Dark Horror by Jeremy Bisho

On the day civilization finally comes to an end, Mia Durante is having brunch with the President of the United States of America. Planes fall and an electromagnetic pulse blinds the United States. Cars crash. Chaos starts …

## 2. Hollowland by Amanda Hocking

The world does not end in a big bang or fire. Nope. It ends with zombies that are knocking at our backdoor. Well, that is how it is, according to this book.

## 1. The Witching Hour by Anne Rice

This is an interesting tale of witchcraft. In this book, we get to meet a nice dynasty of witches.

Those are 5 horror stories. I am sorry that I did not go into too much detail on them, I do not want to give any spoilers and ruin the book for anyone. So, what is your favorite book?
