# 5 Scary Video Games ...

I am going to give you 5 scary video games. These are video games that I have in my collection and enjoy playing from time to time.

## 5. Silent Hill

I think Silent Hill is a scary video game. Do you know what scares me the most about this game? When the sirens start to sound off and Pyramid Head shows up. There is a lot of history behind Pyramid Head and I will tell it to you one day, if you want me to. I like every Silent Hill there is.

## 4. Resident Evil 5

Of course, I like all of the Resident Evil video games. However, I really like number 5 because it is two player. I enjoyed playing this game with my husband. Despite it all, this was a scary movie. I mean, come on, you have zombie like humans running at you. The parts that really made me jump was when I would open a box and a snake would pop out.

## 3. Saw

Yes, I really liked the Saw movies, so you know I am going to list Saw on this list of 5 scary video games. The video game is based on the movie. They are also coming out with Saw 2 the video game.

## 2. Bioshock 1 and 2

These 2 video games are scary. You explorer the underwater city where no one can hear you scream. Don’t worry, because you are not alone. The unexplainable of the Rapture city are stalking your every move.

## 1. Dead Space

Hands down. This is one of the most scary video games EA has ever came out with. It is full of gore, shock and scare. You will be facing aliens that are using corpses to attack you. It has everything from the graphics and atmosphere to the story and fear.

Those are 5 scary video games. What are some of your favorites?
