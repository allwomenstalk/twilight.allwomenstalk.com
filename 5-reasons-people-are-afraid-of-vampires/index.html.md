# 5 Reasons People Are Afraid of Vampires ...

Some people are afraid of things that they cannot see. They are afraid of things that they do not know whether or not are walking on this land. I am going to give you 5 reasons people are afraid of vampires …

## 5. Their Sharp Teeth

Yes, when you look at those sharp teeth, they definitely make you cringe. Those things would make anyone be afraid of them.

## 4. They Can Be Mean

Yes, they can be mean and this is definitely a fact. Sometimes, vampires are hateful. We’re all afraid of hateful things, aren’t we? That’s a good reason to be afraid of vampires.

## 3. They Are Mysterious

Yes, they are definitely mysterious. In fact, you never know if someone is a vampire or not! Oh my! So, when their true identity is finally revealed, we can’t help but to be afraid.

## 2. They Bite

Ouch! Vampires bite and when they bite, they hurt! They love biting the neck. Sure, a bite on the neck while in bed with a partner may be fun, but it’s scary when it’s a bite on the neck from a vampire!

## 1. The Kill

As humans, we are afraid of death. We don’t know what awaits us on the other side. Therefore, it would make sense to be afraid of vampires because they kill. Yes, the vampires on Twilight were good vampires, but there are some out there that love to move in for the perfect kill.

Those are my 5 reasons people are afraid of vampires. What about you? Do you think you are afraid of vampires? What would you do if you came across one?
