# 5 Signs You’re Still Head\-Over\-Heels for Edward Cullen

Are you still dreaming of the Cullens? Below are 5 signs you might be head-over-heels for Edward Cullen. If you relate to all of these, you're definitely a die-hard fan.

## 5. You Wish Your Partner Had Edward's Charm

If you find yourself comparing your partner to Edward, you might be carrying a torch for our favorite vampire. While his old-fashioned charm is undeniably appealing, it's always fun to look back at why we fell for him in the first place. You can read more about the [reasons we all fell in love with Edward Cullen](https://allwomenstalk.com/articles/the-reasons-we-all-fell-in-love-with-edward-cullen/).

## 4. You Can’t Get Enough of His Story

Whether you've read the books countless times or rewatch the films whenever they air, Edward’s story never gets old. If you dream about him at night, you're certainly not alone in this obsession.

## 3. You Sometimes Wish Edward Were Real

We have all faced the cold, hard truth that Edward isn't a real person, and yes, it’s still a little disappointing. It’s a classic sign of a true fan when you occasionally wish that guys like him existed in our world.

## 2. You've Dedicated Space to Him

Be honest: how many pictures of the Cullens have you displayed? Whether you have a designated spot for memorabilia or just a few favorite fan art pieces, having a space dedicated to your favorite character is a classic sign of fandom. ## 1. You Still Rep Team Edward

Whether you write it on your notebooks or declare it in conversation, being on Team Edward is a badge of honor.
