# 5 Moody Songs Every Modern Vampire Would Have on Their Playlist

Are you looking to channel your inner supernatural being? Right now, I am going to start to think from a vampire perspective. I am going to listen to music that I think a vampire would like to listen to. Maybe a vampire likes a variety of different things? From hard rock to moody anthems, the musical taste of the undead is likely as diverse as their history. Below, I am going to give you 5 songs a vampire may like …

| Artist | Song | Genre |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Korn | Alone I Break | Nu Metal |
| Creed | My Own Prison | Post-Grunge |
| Eminem | Not Afraid | Hip Hop |

## 5. Korn – Alone I Break

This is a pretty cool song. In and out of my vampire mind, I enjoy listening to this song. I could so see a vampire driving in his/her car as she is jamming to this song. Can you see it? Yes, if only they really existed … For more dark inspiration, check out our list of [reasons we love Twilight](https://twilight.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-we-all-love-the-twilight-saga/).

## 4. Korn – All in the Family

What’s wrong with this? Perhaps my vampire like mind has sunken into a Korn mood. However, for some reason, I think vampires would really like [Korn](https://www.korn.com/). All in the Family sounds like something a vampire would love to listen to.

## 3. Creed – My Own Prison

I know this is a Christian type band, but for some reason, I think a vampire would like to listen to this song. After all, I enjoy the song and have liked it since I was a child. It captures that feeling of being trapped, much like an eternal life in the shadows.

## 2. Adrienne Frantz – Getaway

This sounds like something a female vampire would listen to. I like this song. [Adrienne Frantz](https://www.adriennefrantz.com/) captured my attention playing on The Young and the Restless. She really does have talent. Talent that a vampire would not pass up. If you're into powerful female leads, you might also enjoy reading about [why we love Bella Swan](https://twilight.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-we-love-bella-swan/).

## 1. Eminem – Not Afraid

Yes, you might be in that place trying to get out. This is definitely a song a vampire would love to listen to. It is also one of my favorites. You can find more of his work on his [official website](https://www.eminem.com/).

- Musical variety is key for an eternal life.

- Rock and Metal often fit the dark aesthetic.

- Don't be afraid to mix genres!

There you have 5 songs a vampire might like. Of course, I left out the classical stuff, but that’s okay. I’m sure there are some rocking vampires out there. What songs do you think vampires would like? Maybe something from the [first point](#5) we mentioned?
