# MUST KNOW: Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart Leave the Love Nest ...

Who is the best couple out there? Step to the side Brangelina, because a new couple has taken us by surprise. We have Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart to watch now.

Rumor has told us that this mega couple has taken the big plunge and are living together. That’s right, it has been reported that they have moved into a home together. Supposedly, they are renting a five bedroom home right outside of Bel-Air.. It has been stated that they are paying fourteen thousand dollars a month. Wow! A MONTH! That is a lot of money to pay in a month, but I am sure they have way more than that.

When I saw that amount, I almost forgot that the main picture here is that Rob and Kristen have moved in together. I was blown away with the thought of them paying that much rent in a month. I know, they must make millions.

**I quote something that one of their friends said:**

““They know how much they love each other, it’s just that when they spend any time apart things can blur that. They’re happy again and so glad to be back together again.”

On another note, I have read reports that Rob and Kristen moved out of their home in LA. Was the rent of fourteen thousand too much to pay? Sorry, I just had to bring that back up. Could it be the fact that everyone was following them and watching them? A release claimed that they no longer rent the home. It was not as private as they wanted it to be.

I think we should mind our own business, like them, be fans as them, but stop stocking them like many are doing. Let them live their life, happy, in private, as a couple. What do you think?
