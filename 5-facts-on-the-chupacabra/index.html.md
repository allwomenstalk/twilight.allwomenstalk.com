# 5 Facts on the Chupacabra ...

Chupacabra means goat sucker. For those of you that do not know me, I have always been big when it comes to the paranormal world. Even as a teenager, I was delving into the paranormal world and always had an interest. That is one of the reason why I have decided to give you 5 facts on the Chupacabra.

## 5. Goat Suckers

Yes, even though they are called goat suckers, they have been accused of killing other animals, such as chickens, horses, pigs and birds. They have even been accused of attacking humans.

## 4. Description

There have been eyewitnesses of this creature. Most of them describe the creature as being a winged, spiny large, creature with red hypnotic eyes.

## 3. Sucking Blood

It is said that they suck blood through the holes that are I their fangs, claws or even their sharp tongues. Of course, we don’t have facts on this one, but this is things that we have heard.

## 2. 1995

During the year 1995, in Florida, sixty nine chickens, goats and ducks were killed. Who did it? They say this creature is to blame.

## 1. It All Started

This legend all started during the year 1987 when Puerto Rican newspapers reported a lot of deaths amongst their livestock. The blood of the animals were drained through circular incisions that were found in their flesh. This reminds me of a giant mosquito.
