# A Biography on Bella Swan \(the Twilight Character, Not Kristen Stewert\)

**Warning:** Spoiler Alerts
We know her as Bella, but her real name is Isabella Marie Swan (that later changed to Cullen). She was born to Renee Dwyer and Charlie Swan on September 13, 1987. Ah, September “13,” there’s that number 13, bad luck, does that explain why she was such a klutz in hr human life?

In Twilight, she is 17 years old. She transfers from Phoenix, Arizona to Forks, Washington. From here, she falls in love with Edward Cullen, who just so happens to be a vampire. In Breaking Dawn, she married Edward and turns into a vampire when she is giving birth to their child. She nearly died while giving birth and turning to a vampire was the only option, but she had already wanted to be changed into a vampire. It’s strange how things work out, isn’t it?

As a human, she had a natural immunity to those special mental powers that certain vampires had. She develops an ability to project a mental shield, which can expand out and protect others from mental powers.

Before Edward, she never had any romantic relationships and she never really fit in the crowd. She briefly took piano lessons, which she complained about. She also used to take ballet lesson, but knowing Bella, that was a disaster. She used to go to Forks, Washington where her dad was every summer, where she would hang out with Jacob. This went on until she was fourteen years old. Then, she put her foot down and no longer wanted to go there. Charlie had to go to California for a couple of weeks each summer instead.

Bella has white skin and auburn hair. There’s a lot o Bella and you know this, because you have read every book of the Twilight series, right?
