# 5 Reasons There Should Be a Twilight Video Game \(besides Scene It\) ...

Why hasn’t Twilight came out as a video game yet? I have heard some talk about it, but that’s all. I think if it came out, it would come out on the Nintendo Wii and the Nintendo DS, which is more targeted to a girl audience. Below, I am going to give you 5 reasons there should be a Twilight video games …

## 5. The Company Would Make Money

Could You imagine how much the company the makes a Twilight video game would make? There is money to be made there and if I could, I would! I know I would make a lot of girls happy!

## 4. It Can Be Interactive

When you play a video game, you are interacting with the characters. Can you imagine how cool it would be to interact and control the characters? You’d get to be Edward and beat Victoria up. Oh, I can see it now!

## 3. Because There’s a Harry Potter Video Game

How fair is this? There’s a Harry Potter video game, so there should be a Twilight video game. It’s time for old Mr. Potter to move over and let Edward have his game time! What do you girls think?

## 2. Girls Would Be Happy

I know that if a Twilight video game came out, plenty of girls, including myself would be happy. This could be just as big as the books.

## 1. It’ll Bring out More Gamers

I believe that it would bring out more gamers. For example, if Twilight came out on the DS, then more girls would be buying a DS just so they could take part in the Twilight video game. After this, they will discover that games are fun and end up playing other games. What do you think?

I know, some of you may not like video games and it may not bother you, but I am a gamer and I believe a game needs to come out! What do you think?
