# 5 Reasons I like Reneesme ...

I was so excited when Edward and Bella found out they were going to have a baby. Actually, I was probably more curious than anything. I wanted to know what it would be like and if it would be a boy or a girl, and so on. I think the outcome was fantastic and here are 5 reasons I like Reneesme.

## 5. Jacob Imprinted on Her

I was so happy when Jacob finally imprinted and I can’t think of a better person for him to imprint on. If Reneesme hadn’t been born, Jacob might have been a lost soul. He’d already lost Bella, in a way, but imprinting on Reneesme sort of enabled him to stay in touch with the entire family without changing himself.

## 4. Everyone Liked Her

It was hard for anyone to come into contact with Reneesme and not fall in love with her. This wasn’t just because she was a baby and most people like babies, but because she had the gift to make people understand.

## 3. She’s Almost Exactly Half Human and Half Vampire

You couldn’t ask for a more equal combination of human and vampire. I thought the characteristics she had from both her parents were perfectly equal.

## 2. She Matures Quickly

I have kids and totally remember the diaper phase. Sure, Edward and Bella didn’t have as long to ‘oo’ and ‘ah’ over Reneesme’s achievements as she made them, but at least the length of time she was basically helpless didn’t last long. It’s very unnerving to know that your child can so easily be hurt. A fast growing youngster greatly reduces this fragile time.

## 1. She Expresses Herself without Words

I thought her ability was the coolest thing ever. To be able to express yourself without having to use words at all is something people strive to do in the real world. However, humans are far away from being able to create pictures in another human’s mind by simply touching his/her face.

I’m sure you have your own reasons for liking Reneesme. I really liked that this character was added to the series. I can’t imagine her not being included. What do you like best about Reneesme? Do you think there is anything that could have been changed about the character to make her even more likeable?
