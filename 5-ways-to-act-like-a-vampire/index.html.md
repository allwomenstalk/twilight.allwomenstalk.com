# 5 Ways to Act like a Vampire ...

I really like vampires. There is just something about these wonderful creatures. They’re mysterious and of course, they are sexy. So, for this blog, I have decided that I would give you 5 ways to act like a vampire …

## 5. Try Not to Blink a Lot

If you really want to act like a vampire, then it is important that you try not to blink a lot. Do you see vampires blinking? Apart from this, you could also stare at people – that one is totally up to you.

## 4. Carry Interesting Things

I don’t know why, but vampires always seemed to carry things that were interesting. Try an antique looking sketchbook, a swiss army knife and a key as a necklace.

## 3. Like Blood

What do vampires really like? They like blood! Therefore, if you are looking to act like a vampire, you will need to like blood. Of course, you don’t want to eat it, but you do need to like it.

## 2. Use Ancient and Unusual Sentences

For example, try some words such as: irrelevant, abysmal and inevitable. If you are good at doing a Romanian accent, then try that too.

## 1. Be Mysterious

Remember that you are no longer a human. You will need to be and act in a mysterious manner. However, at the same time, you should be friendly and relaxed. Learn how to get the vampire attitude down followed by the other five tips I have included above and you will really have people thinking you are a vampire.

Those are 5 ways to act like a vampire. So, if you want to be like a vampire, you should definitely give these a try. Remember, this is going to take some practice, unless, of course, you are a vampire. So, what are some ways you would like to add to this list?
