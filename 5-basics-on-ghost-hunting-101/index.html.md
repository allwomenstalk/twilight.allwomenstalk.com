# 5 Basics on Ghost Hunting 101 ...

Before you go out and hunt ghosts, it is important that you know the 5 basics on ghost hunting 101 that I am about to give you. Write them down, if you have to, but whatever you do, don’t forget what I am about to tell you …

## 5. Know What You’re Doing

First of all, if you are going to call yourself a ghost hunter, you need to know what you are doing. You can’t just go out there and expect to find ghosts without having any knowledge on hunting them down.

## 4. Daylight

Do you know where you plan on hunting the ghost? Well, you need to figure it out so that you can go out in the daylight and check it out. This way, you will be familiar with the area when you go out in the dark.

## 3. Never Go by Yourself

You should know not to go out by yourself, especially in the dark when you are hunting for ghosts.

## 2. The Best Time

The best time to hunt ghosts would be between 9pm and 6am. This is the psychic hours.

## 1. Bring Your I.D

You could end up being questioned by the police if you go to a strange/abandoned area. Therefore, it is important for you to bring your I.D. Besides, you should always have your I.D. on you.

There you have 5 basics on ghost hunting 101. If you enjoy the paranormal world, then you should delve in the world of ghost hunting. You might find that you enjoy it. So, have you ever went hunting for ghosts?
